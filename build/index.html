Our Story

Marley is a small and spunky pup with a picky appetite. Every day, her owner Robin would offer her a new kind of treat. But Marley just didn't like any of the storebought treats. Then one day, Robin decided to make his own dog treats. He started with beef liver snacks. Marley loved them! So Robin tried making more, this time from chicken feet. Marley loved those too! Robin kept making snacks for Marley and Marley's friends at the dog park, experimenting with different ingredients. Finally, Robin decided to start a business to share Marley's favourite snacks with dogs everywhere!
